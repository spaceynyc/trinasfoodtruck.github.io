About Trina's

At Trina's, we believe that the perfect fried fish sandwich starts with the freshest catch of the day. Our founder, Trina, grew up in a coastal town where fresh seafood was a way of life.

Her mission is simple: bring that authentic taste to the streets, using family recipes that have been perfected over generations.

Every sandwich is made to order, ensuring that each bite is as delicious as the last. We source all our ingredients locally whenever possible and are committed to sustainable fishing practices.
